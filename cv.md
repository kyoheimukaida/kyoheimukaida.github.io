## Kyohei Mukaida

## Research interests

* Particle physics in the early Universe
* Reheating, preheating, and thermalization
* Dark matter production
* Baryogenesis, leptogenesis, and lepton flavor
* Electroweak plasma and anomalous transport
* Primordial black holes and gravitational waves
* Cosmological collider physics
* Inflation and scalar fields coupled to gravity
